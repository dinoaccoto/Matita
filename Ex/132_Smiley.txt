# --- Smiley ---

# Colors
put (100, 90, 10) in Yellow
put (0, 0, 0) in Black
put (100, 100, 100) in White

clear graphics

# Face
set color Yellow
circle radius 120 at point(0, 0)

# Face Outline
set color Black
circumference radius 120 thickness 8 at point(0, 0)

# Eyes
set color Black
circle radius 12 at point(-40, 30)
circle radius 12 at point(40, 30)

# Catchlights in the eyes
set color White
circle radius 4 at point(-35, 35)
circle radius 4 at point(45, 35)

# Smile
set color Black
arc radius 60 thickness 8 from (-50, -25) to (50, -25)

frame graphics
